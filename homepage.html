Welcome to Nelson's IIUM

Embark on a culinary adventure right on your campus! Nelson's, the beloved corn in a cup and wafer shop that originated in 1985 from Penang and has since grown into a global sensation with thousands of outlets worldwide, is now open in International Islamic University Malaysia. Experience the rich tapestry of flavors and textures that have made us a household name. Indulge in the perfect combination of savory corn in a cup, adorned with an array of delectable toppings, and crisp, delightful wafers that elevate your snacking experience. Join us in celebrating the joy of good food and camaraderie as Nelson's brings its legacy of taste and innovation right to your doorstep.
